Program Overview

Healthcare Without Barriers

The Telehealth Access Project eliminates geographic and financial barriers to healthcare by providing remote medical consultations, mental health support, and health education through our community health hubs across South Africa.

Using digital technology and community partnerships, we connect patients in underserved areas with qualified healthcare professionals, reducing travel costs and wait times while improving health outcomes.

How It Works

1. Book Appointment

Visit any of our community health hubs or call our hotline to schedule your telehealth consultation. Same-day appointments often available.

2. Video Consultation

Meet with a licensed healthcare provider via secure video call. Our staff assists you with the technology if needed.

3. Get Treatment Plan

Receive diagnosis, treatment recommendations, and prescriptions. Your records are securely stored for follow-up care.

4. Follow-Up Care

Schedule follow-up appointments as needed. We coordinate with local clinics for in-person care when required.
